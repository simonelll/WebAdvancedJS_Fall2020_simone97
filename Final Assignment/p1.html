    "A black cap with a rolling stone tongue symbol. It was a cool cap. I lost it on the driveway outside my apartment and it was ran over by many cars already. I was super sad about that.

    Design Process

    Firstly, I drew the image that I saw in the description: a cap from the side view, and then started to make it.
    The biggest challenge through the process was the rolling stone tongue icon. I tried to use bezier curve, or to load outside images, but they didnâ€™t work well. Then I tried pixel style, calculated pixels, sketched it first(Picture1) and finally worked it out.
    I also added some words to show the lost&found theme.

    Reflection

    1.The language your partner used to describe the image
    It was quite clear, and with lots of emotional words.

    2.Your interpretation of the image
    I was actually thinking of cars running over a falling cap.

    3.The depiction of the image using code
    The interpretation at this stage didn't go very smoothly. Because when I was thinking and sketching, I focused on how it could be like. But I paid more attention to numbers and the components when coding.

    4.The differences in interpretation at all stages of the assignment
    The final outcome is different from what I thought it would be like. Maybe that's how the language and tool we use to communicate have an impact on the content we deliver.
