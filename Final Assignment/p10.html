    Design Process

    API: New York Time

    A description for your translation device. Which feminst data principles did you choose to reference and why?

    This translation device shows the most active reporter from NYT in the related field in the nearest month.
    I want to apply #7 feminist data princinple: Show Labor.

    Reflection

    How does your design respond to feminist data principles?

    Showing labor and references can not only be good for data democracy and transparency, but also good for people to track more qualified informationn through credible sources.

    How did your translation device recontextualize the original datasets?

    I used array to calculate and filter out the reporter who had published the most news articles in the nearest month. This is a comparatively accruate translation of the original data through its simplicity.
