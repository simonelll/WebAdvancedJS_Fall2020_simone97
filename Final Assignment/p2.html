    Design Process

    I posed a question on daily products in this project. The object people thinks the way they are probably has other forms. Also, I had a feeling that clock, a 'container' of time, doesn't look like time in any sense. So I chose to parameterize the clock.
    I drew the sketch first, to see its composition. I decided to divide a clock into three parts: Frame, 3 hands and 12 scales.


    Reflection

    What have you discovered about the form you've chosen through this assignment?
    The most important part in the process is to make every components organized well though their scales are varying. Actually I'd thought to make a water-like clock that has fluid frame and hands and can be put on human bodies, not only on their hands, the parameterized clock can be anywhere. But I believe you can hardly see any of my thoughts in the final work. Maybe next time I should pick an object within my range of ability and focus more on convey its meaning precisely.
