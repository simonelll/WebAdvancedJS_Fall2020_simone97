    A description of your Data Portrait. What was the personal habit you were trying to track?

    I tracked everything I did in the past ten days and categorized them into 8 main parts:

    • Efficiency: Work & Study)
    • Basic: Sleep, Hygenie, Shower, Eat...
    • Freetime: Rest, Read, Do nothing...
    • Social: With friends, Meet people,..
    • FIT: Do sports
    • Waste: A Waste of Time
    • Commute: On a kind of transportation
    • Accident: Things that I didn't expect to happen, for example going to hospital, doing others a favor...

    An instruction for the viewers on how to read your data portrait.

    One round stands for one day (24 hours). There're ten circles, therefore each representing one day in the past ten days. Every color stands for one category.
